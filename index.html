Marketplace Mapview

Website Feature

Adding a map view to The Knot's vendor storefront page is a natural progression from their existing grid layout. A proposed concept, this map view takes from the best of the behaviors of Airbnb and Foursquare's map. In this version, we've only exposed the distance and price in the filter bar and hiding away the rest after learning that these are the most important factors brides consider when looking for a venue. On the left hand side are the venues which you can scroll to look through, while on the right shows the venues on the map after the user started on a search. Functional specs are annotated on the right side.

  • Date: May 2015
  • XO Group
  • Category: UX+UI Design

Everyday Victories

Microsite Campaign

People who don’t have Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA) might wonder why something as simple as walking, baking a pie, or picking up the kids can be a victory. We know better. Living with RA isn’t easy. That’s why every victory should be celebrated. This microsite was created to promote awareness about Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA) and what it means to live with it on a daily basis. Each victory that was shared through text, photo or a pre-existing icon helped achieve $1 towards a charity foundation dedicated to improve the lives of those with RA.



  • Date: March 2013
  • Heartbeat Ideas
  • Category: Concept and Art Direction

Campaign for RLS

Digital Campaign and Branding

After learning that patients with RLS (Restless Leg Syndrome) dread the coming hours of bedtime, we created an online ad campaign calling attention around this idea. The ads ran after the usual bedtime hours and created a 12% growth in traffic for our client's consumer site that had more education and ways around treatment and relief. We also developed the branding for Horizant which was a new treatment coming into the market at the time Go to Consumer Website. Go to HCP Website.

  • Date: January 2014
  • XO Group
  • Category: Digital Campaign and Branding
